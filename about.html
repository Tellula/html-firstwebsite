About me

My name is Julie, I live in France. I just started the Zero to Mastery course. I have no experience whatsoever in coding. I was so scared to try.

I thought i was too old for this (38), not good enough at maths, and overall not intelligent enough.

But after only the HTML part, i find myself having fun, i did not feel the hours i spent on this project, and i'm inspired to try and learn more about everything.

I tried for my first website to play with the functionalities that seemed fun to me on the w3schools website, like the audio, gallery and the navigation bar.

It's still a bit wonky because i'm not sure i understand everything i copied/pasted, especially the navigation bar and the gallery, because it's in CSS and i don't understand a thing about it yet. But i'm proud of it nonetheless.

I love music, movies, and video games, especially Final Fantasy XIV, that's why i decided to make this website by using my favorites screenshots and music from it. I know there have to be some copyright issues, but it's supposed to be for myself, so i should be fine right ?

I am a mother of 3, i'll try to manage my free time to keep on learning the best i can !
